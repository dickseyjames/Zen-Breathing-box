A mindful breathing exercise app for Zen meditation. Practice box breathing to reduce stress, increase focus, and cultivate inner peace.

## Features

- **Box Breathing Technique**: 4-4-4-4 pattern (inhale, hold, exhale, hold for 4 seconds each)
- **Visual Animation**: Calming visual feedback that expands and contracts with your breath
- **Mindful Guidance**: Inspirational quotes to enhance your meditation experience
- **Progress Tracking**: Counts completed breathing cycles
- **Easy Controls**: Start, pause, and reset your breathing session anytime
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click "Start" to begin the breathing exercise
3. Follow the on-screen instructions:
   - **Breathe In** for 4 seconds
   - **Hold** for 4 seconds
   - **Breathe Out** for 4 seconds
   - **Hold** for 4 seconds
4. Use "Pause" to take a break, or "Reset" to start over

## Benefits of Box Breathing

Box breathing is a powerful relaxation technique used by everyone from Navy SEALs to meditation practitioners. It helps:

- Reduce stress and anxiety
- Improve focus and concentration
- Lower blood pressure
- Promote a sense of calm and well-being
- Enhance mindfulness practice
